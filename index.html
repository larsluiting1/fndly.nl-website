FAQ

Common questions

Still have questions? Reach out and we'll get back to you within one business day.

What is AI Search Optimization?
+
AI Search Optimization ensures your business appears and is recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity. As more people use AI to find products and services, being visible in these systems is essential. We optimize your digital footprint so AI models understand and recommend your business accurately.
How long does SEO take to show results?
+
Most clients see meaningful movement in rankings within 60–90 days, with significant traffic growth apparent within 4–6 months. The timeline depends on your current visibility, competition, and services implemented. We provide monthly reports so you always see what's happening.
Can you improve my local search rankings?
+
Absolutely. Local SEO is one of our core specialties. We optimize your Google Business Profile, build consistent citations across directories, manage reviews, and implement geo-targeted content strategies. Many clients reach the Google Maps top 3 within a few months.
What platforms do you optimize for?
+
We optimize across Google Search, Google Maps, Bing, AI search tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude), online directories, social media, and review platforms. Our goal is to maximize your presence everywhere your potential customers search.
How does the free audit work?
+
Book a strategy call and we'll conduct a comprehensive visibility audit covering your website's SEO health, local presence, AI search visibility, review profile, and competitor landscape. You'll receive a clear report with your highest-impact opportunities—whether you become a client or not.
Do you offer monthly retainer services?
+
Yes. Our Growth and Scale plans are ongoing monthly services. SEO and AI search optimization require sustained effort to maintain and improve rankings. We work as an extension of your team—continuously optimizing, reporting, and adapting as search landscapes evolve.
